What is a "smoot"?

A "smoot" is a humorous unit of measurement based off of the height of an MIT fraternity pledge, Oliver Smoot, in 1958.

3 Quick Facts about the "Smoot":

  1. 1 Smoot = 5 ft 7 inches
  2. 1 Smoot = 1.702 meters
  3. The Harvard Bridge is the most famous object measured in smoots, bearing markings along the bridge. It is 364.4 smoots and 1 ear in length.
